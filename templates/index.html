2014 MLB Hits

A visualization of baseball hits throughout the 2014 MLB Season. Hits are displayed as "Hits", "Outs" and "Errors". Hits maybe be navigated via hitter and pitcher combinations. For example putting "Jose Bautista" in the hitter's box (pun intended), will display all hits from "Jose Bautista".

Since the MLB has 30 unique ballparks the dimensions of the drawn park will not be perfect representation of the ballpark which the pitch was hit. The drawn ballpark aims to be a generic size.

Click on the hits to see more information about the hit.

The current dataset is up to date of as early May.
